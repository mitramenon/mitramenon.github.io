Mitra Menon

I started my academic carrer by following my passion for trees and nature and enrolling in a Forestry program during my undergrad. After my undergrad from Kerala Agriculture University I moved to the US, where I pursued my MS under the guidance of Dr. Matt Olson. It was during my MS that I got interested in evolutionary dynamics and understanding how species adapt and respond to changing climatic conditions. Currently I am a PhD candidate in Dr. Andrew Eckert's lab at VCU, where I am investigating the role of introgression in facilitating adaptive evolution in Pines. Please see my Resarch interests for more information about the work I did during my MS and PhD and future interests.
